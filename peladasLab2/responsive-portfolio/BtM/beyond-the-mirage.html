About the Creators

This game was developed by AGIPP IT Solutions as part of a software engineering project. The goal was to create an immersive experience that combines adventure and puzzle-solving elements to engage players while shedding light on the realities of life during the Martial Law era. By drawing from real stories and experiences, the game aims to educate players about this historical period and help counter the spread of misinformation surrounding what is often referred to as the "Golden Era."

About the Game

"Beyond the Mirage" invites players to uncover the hidden truths of the Marcos era. A mirage, an optical illusion, symbolizes how the “Golden Era” may appear one way but hides deeper complexities and distorted realities. The game encourages players to explore these untold stories and challenge misconceptions. This desktop-based Historical Top-Down RPG allows players to navigate a historical setting, shape the narrative, and interact with in-game characters, making choices that influence the course of the story.

My Contribution to the Game

As the sole developer of this project, I was responsible for every aspect of the game, from the initial concept to the final release. Below are the key areas I contributed to:

Game Concept & Design

I conceptualized the entire story and mechanics, focusing on Cebuano culture and traditions. The narrative, visuals, and gameplay are all designed to provide a unique experience for players.

Using my programming skills, I implemented the core game features, including the AR elements and gameplay mechanics. I also handled the integration of assets and optimization for smooth performance.

Art & Visual Assets

Testing & Debugging

As the only developer, I was also responsible for testing the game, identifying bugs, and optimizing the game for a seamless experience across different devices.
