About the Creators

This game was developed by AGIPP IT Solutions as part of their software engineering project. The aim of the project was to combine adventure and puzzle-solving elements to offer players a distinct gaming experience. The game was created to raise awareness and help prevent the spread of misinformation during the Martial Law period, also known as the Golden Era.

About the Game

Beyond the Mirage is an adventure game that takes place in a vast and mystical world. Players will embark on a journey to solve puzzles, overcome challenges, and uncover the mysteries hidden within the game world. Featuring a unique art style, engaging storyline, and dynamic gameplay, this game aims to captivate players and keep them immersed in its story.
