About the Game

In a world where cats of all types—mages, swordsmen, knights, and rogues—are captured and forced into a brutal arena, only the deadliest can claim victory. These skilled cats must rely on their unique abilities and ruthless instincts to eliminate every rival standing in their way. Each battle is a savage fight for dominance, and only the fiercest feline will emerge victorious, while the rest are doomed to a grim fate: being turned into siomeow. Siomeow Legends is a top-down, free-for-all deathmatch game where survival isn’t enough—players must dominate the battlefield by defeating as many opponents as possible to secure the top spot. Set in randomized arenas filled with hidden dangers and cunning traps, players control fierce feline champions, fighting tooth and claw to rack up kills and climb the leaderboard.

My Contribution to the Game

As part of a five-person team, I contributed to various aspects of Beyond the Mirage, a role-playing game developed using Unity. My work focused primarily on quality assurance, front-end design, and visual asset creation.

Quality Assurance & Testing

Served as the Lead QA Engineer and primary tester, writing and executing over 200 test cases. I ensured the game's high standards by identifying bugs, verifying features, and refining gameplay through detailed feedback.

Game Development

Collaborated with the team to develop core game systems in Unity using C#, including scripting for the in-game dialogue system and assisting with scene management.

Art & Visual Design

Created and refined over 70 visual assets such as sprites, tilesets, and animated cutscenes using Aseprite, Krita, and Unity's animation tools. Contributed to establishing a consistent visual style throughout the game.

UI/UX & Wireframing

Designed wireframes in Figma to guide UI/UX development, helping ensure smooth navigation from the home screen to in-game interactions. Worked closely with the team to maintain an intuitive user experience.

Project Workflow

Participated in agile development using SCRUM methodology, attending regular sprints and stand-up meetings to iterate on features, prioritize tasks, and support efficient team collaboration.
